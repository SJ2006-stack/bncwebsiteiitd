Business and Consulting Club, IIT Delhi

IIT Delhi's premier undergraduate business organization

BCC is organized into three initiatives: Finance, Consulting, and Entrepreneurship. Once invited to join BCC, we develop members' skills and prepare them for the workplace through our intensive, semester-long, Board Education Program.

Explore Initiatives

Business Strategies

Business Strategies

Our business strategies focus on fostering innovation, collaboration, and sustainable growth. We strive to create unique value for our members through skill-development workshops, hands-on projects, and industry exposure while building strong networks with alumni and partners.

  • Innovation First
  • Collaboration & Networking
  • Sustainability & Growth
  • Community Impact

Consulting

The Consulting Initiative connects our members to top consulting firms. We partner with local start-ups and established firms on strategy projects.

  • Case Studies
  • Strategy Projects
  • Client Consulting
  • Industry Analysis

Entrepreneurship

We work to spur entrepreneurial ventures among IIT Delhi undergraduates through speaker series and mentorship programs.

  • Speaker Series
  • Startup Incubation
  • Pitch Competitions
  • Mentorship Programs
