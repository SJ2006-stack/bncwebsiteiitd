Business and Consulting Club, IIT Delhi

IIT Delhi's premier undergraduate business organization

BCC is organized into three initiatives: Finance, Consulting, and Entrepreneurship. Once invited to join BCC, we develop members' skills and prepare them for the workplace through our intensive, semester-long, Board Education Program.

Explore Initiatives

Our Initiatives

Finance

Members in the Finance Initiative participate in mock trading and an educational program comprising the fundamentals of valuation, global markets, economics, and trading.

  • Mock Trading
  • Valuation Analysis
  • Market Research
  • Investment Strategies

Consulting

The Consulting Initiative connects our members to top consulting firms. We partner with local start-ups and established firms on strategy projects.

  • Case Studies
  • Strategy Projects
  • Client Consulting
  • Industry Analysis

Entrepreneurship

We work to spur entrepreneurial ventures among IIT Delhi undergraduates through speaker series and mentorship programs.

  • Speaker Series
  • Startup Incubation
  • Pitch Competitions
  • Mentorship Programs

Board Education Program

New club members learn fundamental business concepts in finance, consulting, and entrepreneurship during our intensive semester-long program.
